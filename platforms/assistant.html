AlohaAssistant

Your voice-enabled AI executive assistant.

A 24/7 virtual exec assistant that answers your phone, books appointments, takes messages, and handles calls with natural voice AI. Never miss a customer again.

Request Early Access See All Platforms

AlohaAssistant is a voice-first AI that acts as your business's virtual executive assistant. It answers phone calls, schedules appointments on your calendar, routes urgent matters to the right person, takes detailed messages, and logs every conversation — all in a natural-sounding voice that represents your brand, 24/7.
